Delete Your Account

You can delete your Travel Buddy account directly from within the app at any time. No need to contact support.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Travel Buddy app and sign in.
  2. Tap the Settings tab (bottom right).
  3. Scroll down to the Account section.
  4. Tap Delete account.
  5. Confirm the deletion in the prompt that appears.
⚠️ This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

What data is deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes all of the following from our servers immediately:

  • Your profile (name, email, avatar)
  • All trips, notes, and trip photos
  • Your visited countries list
  • All friend connections

No data is retained after deletion.

Subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active subscription. To avoid future charges, cancel your subscription before deleting your account:

  • iOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Travel Buddy → Cancel
  • Android: Google Play → Subscriptions → Travel Buddy → Cancel

Need help?

If you're unable to access the app, contact us at travel_buddy_info@proton.me and we'll delete your account manually within 30 days.
