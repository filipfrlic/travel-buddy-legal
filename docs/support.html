Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a visited country?

Tap any country on the map to mark it as visited. You can also browse the Countries tab and tap a country from the list.

How do I add a trip?

Go to the Trips tab and tap the "+" button. Select the country, set your travel dates, and optionally add notes.

How do I add friends?

Open your profile and tap "Add Friend" to show your QR code. Ask your friend to scan it from their app, or scan theirs.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data — trips, visited countries, and friends. This action cannot be undone.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot password?" and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link shortly.

Is my data backed up?

Yes — your data is synced to our secure servers when you're signed in. If you reinstall the app or switch devices, simply sign in to restore everything.
